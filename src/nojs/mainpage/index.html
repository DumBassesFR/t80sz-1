Welcome to The 80s Zone!

Born from the ashes of the official I am Jemboy Discord server,
The 80s Zone has been a social hangout for hundreds of people
since its public launch on July 4th, 2018.

So where did the community originate from? It all started on September 20th, 2017
when mashup youtuber I am Jemboy had the idea of creating a public hangout
for fans and creators alike.

In his mind, it would be a relaxed environment where everyone could be friendly
and creative whilst running the server with a hands off approach.
Unfortunately, on September 29th, 2017, reality hit.

The Bear Zone as it was called was a place centered around the idea of chaos
and teenage humor, making it a place rife for drama. Though there were issues that persisted
throughout its 9 months, a tightknit community was born out of the hellish environment.

With a high staff turnover rate and neglect from an owner
that had seemingly abandoned the place, it was up to the community
to fend for itself and become self sustainable.

So on July 4th, 2018, The Bear Zone was shut down via a battle royale
and retooled to become an archive while The 80s Zone became a fresh start for the community,
owned by D Vana until February 21st, 2019.

Skelly from that point on became owner and essentially demoted himself to the level
of a mod, freeing the server from any real ownership.

It has since been several years and the community goes on,
acting in a way that's starkly different from the days of Fall 2017.

Despite its name, The 80s Zone
has very little to actually do with the 1980's!

The 80s Zone does however, represent a group of people dedicated to creativity, spending time with one
another, and spreading the word of the community.

For reference, in 2020, The 80s Zone pulled a total of 1.57 million messages in conversation, images,
and other pieces of media shared! There's a lot to enjoy with the server whether it's the anime community in #toonami,
the technology community in #informix, or the large art community found throughout the Westnet category.

If you're interested in our very deep wealth of history,
we have pages upon pages of content that tell the tale of how we got here
alongside other bonus pages we felt needed to be preserved.
So start reading and please enjoy!
